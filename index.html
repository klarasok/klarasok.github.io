Hello Gorgeous, "We're Beautiful" is a photographic celebration of the many forms, colors and shapes that beauty takes and makes life what it is. It is a collection of photographic memories I created along the way of the many travels that have enriched my life until today. Here you'll find people, landscapes, urban or celestial, that are making life diverse and beautiful.
